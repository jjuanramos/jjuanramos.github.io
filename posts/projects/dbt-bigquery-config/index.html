I wanted something akin to what Jeremy Cohen layed out on his now classic article: setting up different warehouses (or databases) for different purposes, having dev and prod environments, as well as an environment where the raw data existed, independently of the other two. This provides Role Based Access by default and a clean structure.

Jeremy Cohen’s article was focused on Snowflake, mine’s tried to adapt his to BigQuery. I think it turned out okay, with ~3k views as of now.
